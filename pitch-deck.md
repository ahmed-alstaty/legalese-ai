## Slide 1: The Problem
### Everyday people and small businesses can't understand legal documents

- **68% of people don't read** contracts and terms of service before signing
- **Legal language is intentionally complex** - optimized for precision, not comprehension
- **Professional legal help is expensive** and inaccessible for most
- **Results in poor decisions, financial risk, and costly mistakes**

*"This document feels like it's written to confuse me. I'm worried I'll miss something important that could cost me."* - Typical user sentiment
